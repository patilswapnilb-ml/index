1. General Return Guidelines

  • Products can be returned within a specified timeframe (7 to 30 days) from the delivery date, depending on the product category (detailed below).
  • To be eligible for return, items must be in unused, undamaged condition with all original tags, packaging, and accessories.

2. Category-Specific Return Timelines and Conditions

Electronics and Appliances

  • Return Window: 7 days from delivery.
  • Conditions:
    • Returns accepted for manufacturing defects, physical damage during transit, or incorrect product delivery.
    • Items must include original packaging, manuals, accessories, and invoice.
    • For large appliances (TVs, refrigerators, washing machines, etc.), installation/technician reports may be required.

Clothing, Footwear, and Accessories

  • Return Window: 10 days from delivery.
  • Conditions:
    • Products must be unused, unwashed, and returned with original tags and packaging.
    • Footwear must be tried on indoors and returned without wear or tear.

Books, Media, and Office Supplies

  • Return Window: 7 days from delivery.
  • Conditions:
    • Returns accepted for physical damage or delivery of an incorrect item.
    • Digital and downloadable products are non-returnable.

Personal Care, Health, and Hygiene Products

  • Return Window: Non-returnable (exceptions for damaged/defective items).
  • Conditions:
    • Products like cosmetics, health supplements, and hygiene-related items cannot be returned due to safety concerns.

3. Non-Returnable Products

  • Software, digital downloads, and subscriptions.
  • Gift cards and promotional vouchers.
  • Products sold during clearance sales or marked as "Final Sale."
  • Customized and personalized items.

4. How to Initiate a Return

  • Visit the My Orders section on our website or app.
  • Select the item and reason for return.
  • Upload supporting photos if required.
  • Choose your preferred resolution:
    • Refund: Amount refunded to the original payment method.
    • Replacement: A new product is shipped to you.
  • Pack the item securely and hand it over to our delivery partner during pick-up.
